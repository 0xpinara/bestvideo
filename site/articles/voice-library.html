Find the perfect community voice

1,000+ voices, filtered by source, gender, and tag. The fastest way to discover a voice you'll actually keep.

Beginner 3 min read Updated 2026-05-25

The Voice Library is the discovery layer for voices. Search, filter, preview, and add any voice to your projects.

Filter strategically

  • Trending sorts by recent use — these are voices that creators are picking right now.
  • Community shows voices shared by other creators — wider stylistic range than the designed catalogue.
  • Designed shows the curated Lumen catalogue — quality-vetted, every voice tested across languages.

Tag chips

The tag row below the filters cross-cuts: narration, cinematic, casual, storybook, documentary, energetic, soft, deep, warm, anime. Combine a category filter with a tag for surgical discovery.

Preview before you commit

Tap the play button. Spend 30 seconds previewing 5 voices before you choose. The right voice changes the whole feeling of your content.
