Add a precision voiceover to any video

Multi-segment narration with start and end times, a different voice per segment, and music ducking that doesn't fight the read.

Beginner 5 min read Updated 2026-05-25

Voiceover differs from Faceless Video in one important way: you bring your own footage. Lumen lays narration over it, perfectly timed, with the music tucked underneath.

Upload footage

Any aspect ratio works. The narrative track is created independently and re-merged at the end.

Plan your segments

A segment is one continuous narration. Most short videos have 2-5 segments, separated by visual beats. Sketch them out:

  • Segment 1 (0:00 - 0:05): hook.
  • Segment 2 (0:08 - 0:15): promise.
  • Segment 3 (0:20 - 0:32): payoff.

Leave gaps between segments. Silence is part of the narration.

Voice per segment

This is the secret weapon. Pick one warm voice for the hook and a different, more authoritative voice for the payoff — your video will feel like a conversation between two friends. Or pick a calm voice for explanation and an energetic voice for the call to action.

Duck background music

Leave this on. Lumen drops the music by 9dB whenever a segment plays, then brings it back up. Sounds professional, takes zero effort.

Remove original audio

Use this when your footage has unwanted sound — dialogue you're replacing, wind noise, distracting background. The original sound is removed and only your narration + music remain.
