Cut your subject from any video

Transparent, green screen, or AI-replaced background — and how to get a clean edge on every shot.

Beginner 4 min read Updated 2026-05-25

Background Remover does frame-by-frame rotoscoping at the speed of a coffee. Three output options:

  • Transparent — exports with alpha channel. Drop into any video editor and place on a new background.
  • Green screen — exports with a clean green key. For editors that need a chroma source.
  • Replace with AI — Lumen generates a new background from a prompt and composites it under your subject in one step.

Edge feather

Higher feather = softer edge. Lower = harder cut. Default 0.4 is balanced. Push to 0.7 for shots with frizzy hair or fur. Drop to 0.2 for graphic-style sharp cuts.

What works best

Single-subject shots, clean lighting, good colour separation between subject and background. Lumen handles crowds too, but the edges get softer.
