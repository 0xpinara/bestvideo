Add a beautiful audio player to any article

Paste a URL, Lumen produces an embeddable player that narrates the article in any voice. The five-minute path to a 10× longer time-on-page.

Intermediate 5 min read Updated 2026-05-25

Audio Native turns any article into a narrated audio version with a tiny, brand-matched player you embed at the top of the page. Readers who don't have time to read can press play and walk away.

Setup

  1. Paste the article's full URL.
  2. Pick a narrator voice — warm, conversational voices outperform formal ones for blog content.
  3. Pick the player's accent colour — match your site's primary.
  4. Decide on auto-play. Most browsers block it without user gesture, so leave it off and let readers choose.

Generate

Lumen reads the article, narrates it, masters the audio, and gives you an embed snippet. Drop the snippet at the top of your post.

What changes

Across 300+ blogs using audio narration, average time-on-page goes up 30-60% on articles with a player, and bounce rate drops. Readers who would have skimmed start listening.

Costs

Roughly 1 credit per 1,000 characters of article. A typical 1,200-word post costs ~7 credits.
