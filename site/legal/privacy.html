Privacy

A short, honest privacy promise.

No tricks, no surprises. Last updated 25 May 2026.

What we store

  • Your account email and display name.
  • A list of the projects you've made (titles, settings, status).
  • The generated outputs you choose to save to the cloud.

What lives only on your device

  • Your in-app drafts and history (until you sync them).
  • Your ElevenLabs / OpenAI / Runway API keys (in your phone's secure enclave; never sent to us).
  • Uploaded source media until you generate (we stream it directly to the provider).

What we never store

  • Your raw prompts after generation completes (they live with the model provider only).
  • Anything we'd be embarrassed to email you about.

Who we share with

The model provider you choose (Veo, Sora, Runway, ElevenLabs, etc.) gets the data needed to render your generation. Nothing else.

Delete everything

Sign in, open You → Settings → Delete account. Everything goes immediately and irreversibly. Email hello@lumenstudio.app if you'd like a written confirmation.

Children

Lumen Studio is not directed at children under 13. We don't knowingly collect data from anyone in that age range.

Contact

Reach the privacy team at privacy@lumenstudio.app.
