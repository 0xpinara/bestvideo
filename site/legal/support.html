Common questions

Why are my generations slow?

Peak hours (16:00 - 21:00 UTC) queue more requests. Pro and Studio plans use a priority queue and skip the wait.

How do I cancel?

You → Subscription → Cancel. Your plan stays active until the end of the billing period; you can re-subscribe anytime without losing your library.

Can I get a refund?

Yes, within 14 days of any purchase, for any reason. Email us.

Is commercial use allowed?

Yes, on Pro and Studio plans. The Free plan is for personal, non-commercial use.
