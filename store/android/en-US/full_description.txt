🎥 AI VIDEO GENERATOR (Text to Video)
Describe any scene and watch it come alive. Pick your model, aspect ratio (16:9, 9:16, 1:1 and more), duration, resolution up to 1080p, and audio. Use a reference image as a start or end frame.

📱 FACELESS VIDEO MAKER
Type an idea, pick a voice and a B-roll style — cinematic, animated, documentary, or hand-drawn. We write the script, narrate, edit, and burn in kinetic captions automatically. Perfect for YouTube Shorts, Reels, and TikTok.

💬 AUTO CAPTIONS (Subtitle Generator)
Upload any video, get accurate captions in seconds. Karaoke, kinetic, bold, minimal, or subtle styles. 30+ languages. Custom highlight colors, font sizes, and positions. Optional emoji suggestions.

🌍 AI DUBBING (Voice Translation)
Translate any video into 30+ languages while preserving the original speaker's voice and emotion. Lip-sync, multi-speaker support, and background-music preservation included.

🎙 AI VOICEOVER
Drop precision-timed narration onto any video, segment by segment. Different voice per segment. Auto-ducks background music while the narrator speaks.

🎶 VIDEO TO MUSIC
Auto-score your video by genre, mood, intensity, and instrumentation. Lumen reads your cuts and writes a melody that breathes with the scene.

🎧 AI VOICE GENERATOR (Text to Speech)
Studio-grade text-to-speech with 12+ natural voices. Stability, similarity, style exaggeration, and speaker-boost controls give you Hollywood-quality narration from any phrase.

🎙 LONG-FORM AUDIO & PODCASTS
Multi-speaker scripts with a different voice for every character. Perfect for podcasts, audiobooks, dialogues, YouTube essays, and bedtime stories.
