AI Video Maker · Make Faceless Videos turns a single sentence into a finished, cinematic video — with voice, captions, music, and dubbing in 30+ languages. Built for creators who want the power of a studio in their pocket, without the learning curve.

Whether you make Reels, Shorts, TikToks, YouTube videos, ads, explainers, lessons, or bedtime stories — every tool you need to ship is one tap away.

— WHAT YOU CAN MAKE —

• AI Video Generator — Describe any scene and watch it come alive. Choose your model, aspect ratio, duration, resolution, and audio. Use a reference photo as a start or end frame for total control.

• Faceless Video Maker — Type an idea, pick a voice and a B-roll style (cinematic, animated, documentary, or hand-drawn). Lumen writes, narrates, edits, and burns in kinetic captions for you.

• Auto Captions — Upload any clip and get accurate, beautifully styled captions in seconds. Karaoke, kinetic, bold, minimal, or subtle styles. 30+ languages, custom highlight colors, emoji suggestions.

• AI Dubbing — Translate any video into 30+ languages while preserving the original speaker's voice and emotion. Lip-sync, multi-speaker, and background-music preservation included.

• AI Voiceover — Drop precision-timed narration onto any video, segment by segment. Choose a different voice per segment. Auto-ducks the background music.

• Video to Music — Auto-score your video by mood, genre, intensity, and instrumentation. Lumen reads your cuts and writes a melody that breathes with the scene.

• AI Voice Generator (TTS) — Studio-grade text-to-speech with 12+ voices, stability, similarity, style, and speaker-boost controls. From whispered narration to confident ads.

• Long-Form Audio & Podcasts — Multi-speaker scripts with a different voice per character. Perfect for podcasts, audiobooks, dialogues, and YouTube essays.
